About Us - Dear Tokyo Japanese Restaurant

Welcome to Dear Tokyo, where authentic Japanese cuisine meets a warm, inviting ambiance in the heart of Yangon. Situated amidst the vibrant cityscape, we bring the essence of Japan to your table, offering a delightful fusion of traditional flavors and contemporary dining experiences.

Our Story

Dear Tokyo began with a passion for sharing the rich tapestry of Japanese culinary artistry with the people of Myanmar. Established by a team of dedicated food enthusiasts, our journey started with a vision to create a haven where food lovers could immerse themselves in the genuine tastes of Japan without leaving Yangon.

Cuisine

At Dear Tokyo, we pride ourselves on crafting an extensive menu curated to tantalize your taste buds. From meticulously prepared sushi rolls that showcase the freshest ingredients to sizzling teppanyaki dishes that ignite your senses, each creation embodies the essence of Japanese gastronomy. Our chefs, trained in the intricate techniques of Japanese cooking, ensure every dish reflects authenticity and excellence.

Community and Beyond
